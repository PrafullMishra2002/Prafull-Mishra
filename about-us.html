ABOUT US

We would like to warmly welcome you toKapoor Education Academy(School). Choosing your child's school is such an important decision. Your child has already started an a learning journey and we see our role as being partners with you in steering your child along that journey. Kapoor Education Academy(School) is a unique school exclusively for childeren's, an outcome the deep aspiration, values and expertise of its Director, Mr. Pankaj Kapoor.

SCHOOL MOTTO

Our School motto is, learn and grow. We strive to create a safe , positive and inspiring learning environment, where each children's develops a love of learning and an enquring mind. Our engaging, creative curriculam sets out to challange and learning opportunities. The overall growth of our students is our only priority.

OUR AIMS & OBJECTIVE

1. To develop the potential of every children's in all aspects and make them educated in the true sense of the word.

2. To provide an enriched and open environment for higher level interaction.

3. To maintain a higher degree of discipline.

4. To encourage and promote humanitarian values of love, justice, tolerance, respect, honesty, service and trust throughout the school community.

5. To impact critical skills rather than giving a lot of information.

6. To offer stimulating, inquiry-based, enjoyable learning suited to the varying needs of the children's.

7. To provide wider opportunities and responsibilities for learning, understanding, service and participation in the community life offered by the school.

8. To create a community in which all members are fully involved through the development of harmonious relationship between management, parents, staff and student.
