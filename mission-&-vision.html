Vision Of The School

Kapoor Education Academy(school) endeavours to create a school atmosphere that focuses on care and holistic development of students.


WE AIM TO-
i). Strive for academic excellence.
ii). Upgrade our infracture in technology and stay abreast with the emagrging trends.
iii). Foster creativity and innovation through involvement in art and craft, music, dance and theatre.
iv). promote good presenting skill among parents and those in a parenting role.


We aspire to continue with greater fervour to build foundation of our values- LOVE, PEACE, TRUTH, NON-VIOLENCE and RIGHT CONDUCT.
These values will help to envolve a balanced and tolerant being who can find fulfillment within and contribute to the world around.



Mission Of the School

The Mission of the Kapoor Education Academy(school) is to promote human excellence in every students by balancing character development and academic achivements.

The school aims evolving every students potential by drawing out the infinite intelligence and goodness talent in every individual through emphasis on human values of TRUTH, NON-VIOLENCE, RIGHT CONDUCT, LOVE and PEACE.
We aspire to be a centre of all-round excellence in education where individual talents of students are discovered and nurtured and they creatively construct their own knowledge and widsom, thereby guided to acquire and assimilate the competencies and skill needed for begin fruitful global citizen of the world.

The school aims to uphold the founder's commitments to excellence in all field with emphasis on its motto 'Learn and Grow' and to prepare the students for the 21st century.
